How Environmental Policies Help Protect Canada's Future

By Darshdeep Singh | CSCI 101.s11

Introduction

Canada uses many environmental policies to protect nature, reduce pollution, and prepare for climate change. This website explains some key examples and their impact.

Key Policies

  • Carbon Pricing – Putting a cost on pollution to reduce emissions.
  • Single-Use Plastic Ban – Banning items like plastic straws and bags.
  • Clean Energy Support – Funding wind, solar, and hydro power.

Challenges

Some policies face pushback from industries, and some areas still rely on fossil fuels. There's also a need for stronger enforcement in certain regions.

Conclusion

Environmental policies are important for a healthy future. While Canada has made progress, continued effort is needed to ensure long-term sustainability.
